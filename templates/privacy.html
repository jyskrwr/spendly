Last updated: June 28, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account with Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly — such as your name and email address. When you use the Service, we also collect the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you add.

We may additionally collect basic technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and device information to help us operate and improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Service. Specifically, your account information is used to authenticate you and personalise your experience, and your expense data is used to generate the summaries and reports you see in the app.

We may use anonymised, aggregated data — with no personally identifiable information — to analyse usage patterns and improve the product. We will never sell your personal data or use it for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us through the app. We will action such requests within 30 days.

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third-party services for marketing or analytics purposes. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the Service, and these providers process your data only on our behalf and under strict confidentiality obligations.

Any links to external websites from within the app are provided for convenience only. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the app. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.

By using Spendly, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy. Please also review our Terms and Conditions.
